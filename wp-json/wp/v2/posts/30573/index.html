In our rapidly evolving technological world, artificial intelligence (AI) remains at the forefront, constantly pushing boundaries and transforming various industries. One of the most captivating branches of AI is generative AI, a technology that can autonomously generate content, artwork, code, and many other creations. <\/p>\n\n\n\n

Within a few months, ChatGPT and other generative artificial intelligence (AI) tools have proved that they can easily integrate into software developers’ workflows and offer immediate increase in productivity. <\/p>\n\n\n\n

In this blog post, we will explore the most popular generative AI tools and platforms, along with their key features.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

What is Generative AI?<\/strong><\/h2>\n\n\n\n

Generative artificial intelligence is a category of AI technology that can generate diverse content. GenAI uses algorithms and models to create various forms of content, such as texts, images, audio, video, and more.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

What are Generative AI Tools?<\/strong><\/h2>\n\n\n\n

GenAI tools operate on machine-learning techniques and deep-learning models to create multiple forms of content, including text, images, video, and more. These software applications can use AI capabilities to generate content and can be used as assistants, via APIs, or within search engines.\u00a0<\/p>\n\n\n\n

What are <\/strong>Generative AI Platforms<\/strong>?<\/strong><\/h2>\n\n\n\n

GenAI platforms provide a wide range of services, including multiple tools, models, and other components within their ecosystem. GenAI platforms offer comprehensive capabilities that are highly beneficial for AI development and deployment projects. Nonetheless, it is noteworthy that the terms “platform” and “tool” are frequently used interchangeably due to the overlap in their use.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

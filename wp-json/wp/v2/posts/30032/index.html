Before moving forward, let’s have a quick introduction to Stacksync. <\/p>\n\n\n\n

Stacksync is a no-code platform that simplifies CRM integration by providing seamless synchronization between your CRM and databases\/data warehouses. It eliminates the need for manual data entry and ensures that your information is always updated across all your systems.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Understanding Stacksync Functionalities <\/strong><\/h2>\n\n\n\n

Stacksync uses a no-code setup that allows you to connect your apps and choose the tables to sync in just minutes. Its real-time streaming pipeline ensures that updates propagate in milliseconds, while its bidirectional sync feature enables instant data updates between your CRM and databases. <\/p>\n\n\n\n

With real-time and two-way sync capabilities, Stacksync allows you to easily connect your CRM like Salesforce, Hubspot, or SAP with databases, such as Postgres or Google BigQuery in a few minutes. This two-way synchronization, Stacksync ensures that any updates made in your CRM or databases are quickly reflected in the other system, which keeps your data consistent and accurate across all your platforms.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Bubble<\/a> is the most powerful no-code platform<\/a> that has grown remarkably in recent years. It is regarded as the top choice for creating production-ready web apps.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

If you are new to Bubble, we recommend you to read our comprehensive blog on \u201cWhat is Bubble<\/a>.\u201d<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Examples of Apps Built with Bubble.io<\/strong><\/h2>\n\n\n\n

1.<\/strong> TicketRev<\/a><\/strong>: An Event Ticketing Marketplace<\/h3>\n\n\n\n

It is an event ticketing marketplace that allows fans to submit bids for event tickets at their desired price and seating location, allowing sellers to accept the bid and sell their tickets right away.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

This Bubble-built platform assists fans in searching for the events they wish to attend. It suggests a fair price range so users know if their offer is reasonable. When a seller accepts the bid, TicketRev lets them sell discreetly and get payment instantly without needing to list their tickets for sale.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

2.<\/strong> Workello<\/a><\/strong>: A Talent Recruitment Portal<\/h3>\n\n\n\n

Businesses looking to hire content creators often find the process of sourcing, evaluating, testing, and hiring writers to be time-consuming. That\u2019s why the content team at Workello took the initiative to create an ideal system to quickly identify and hire skilled writers. The team has now made this proprietary system available as an app for anyone to use.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Workello is a skill-testing platform that assists companies in finding and hiring the top 1% of writer applicants. Using the automated Workello dashboard, companies can efficiently centralize candidates. It allows them to assess candidates through pre-hire skills tests, reject applicants, conduct interviews, and send offers to candidates with just a single click. Their Bubble-built app simplifies the process of discovering exceptional talent.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

3.<\/strong> Yusa<\/strong><\/a>: Home Services Marketplace<\/h3>\n\n\n\n

Many homeowners find it tough to find quick and reliable expert help for home repairs, maintenance, and improvement. Yusa created a mobile, two-sided marketplace app that enables people to connect with licensed experts through video, eliminating the hassle of hiring expensive and time-consuming companies attempting DIY solutions.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Yusa’s innovative Bubble-built platform enables homeowners to share their details about the issue so they get matched with licensed experts, who can offer rapid, high-quality service through video chat. Yusa manages all transactions, ensuring that the majority of the earned revenue goes directly to the experts.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

4.<\/strong> Playground IEP<\/a><\/strong>: An Educational Service Portal<\/h3>\n\n\n\n

A portal for managing special caseloads in school. The concept originated from Sean Klamm, a special educator who wants to simplify the manual tasks of case management. The objective is to shift the focus from spreadsheets to students. It gives teachers a convenient space to access current student information and streamlines automated IEP (Individualized Education Program).<\/p>\n\n\n\n

The portal offers three time-saving features for school users. Firstly, there is an IEP snapshot that makes it easy to create, edit, share, and view information for each child on the caseload. Secondly, the portal offers automated IEP scheduling that automatically invites each student\u2019s service provider to meetings. Lastly, there is an individualized teacher dashboard that displays all students on a teacher\u2019s caseload for efficient monitoring and management.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

5.<\/strong> Good Gigs<\/strong><\/a>: A Career Platform<\/h3>\n\n\n\n

This application connects job seekers with mission-driven companies offering full-time, part-time, and remote opportunities. This platform was developed using Bubble by Dale Wilkinson.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

This platform includes a community where purpose-driven professionals can connect and interact. Members receive weekly updates on exciting opportunities directly in their inbox.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

This app is an excellent marketplace for freelancers and job seekers, providing them with a platform to use their skills for positive change with socially responsible companies. Moreover, it also serves as a valuable resource for the organization to find talent that aligns with its values.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

6.<\/strong> Aware Health<\/a><\/strong>: A Healthcare Platform<\/h3>\n\n\n\n

It is a digital healthcare platform designed to offer convenient and personalized medical care to employees. The objective of this app is to make healthcare accessible by providing various services such as same-day appointments and thorough assessments. It not only saves time but also conserves energy and resources.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Aware Health evaluates various aspects of well-being, including sleep habits, social health, fitness, nutrition, and stress levels. This assessment allows users to create their care plans, addressing underlying factors that might require modification to work duties. This platform allows users to proactively manage their health.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Built using Bubble, the platform enables the integration of healthcare modules without complex coding. It simplifies the process of delivering high-quality health services with strong backend development. Users can choose a monthly subscription, reducing annual costs and providing peace of mind with stable rates. In essence, employees can access timely care without traveling to the clinic.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

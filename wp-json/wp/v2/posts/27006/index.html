A Brief Introduction to Shopify <\/strong><\/h2>\n\n\n\n

Shopify is a subscription-based CMS<\/a> that offers an easy way to create an online store<\/a> and sell their products. This platform provides a flexible solution for Shopify store owners to sell their products in person using the Shopify POS (point-of-sale) application and compatible hardware. <\/p>\n\n\n\n

For Shopify merchants<\/a> who operate both online and physically, the platform offers automatic synchronization of inventory and stock, so you can effortlessly manage all your store operations from a single account, accessible to any device.
